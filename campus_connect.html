Campus Connect ERP

Campus Connect ERP is a comprehensive software solution designed to streamline and manage educational institution operations. It integrates modules for student information, admissions, attendance, fee management, and academic progress tracking. With user-friendly dashboards, it enhances communication between administrators, teachers, students, and parents. This ERP system helps optimize efficiency, ensure data accuracy, and support decision-making in campuses.

Features:
  • Student Information System
  • Admission Management
  • Fee Management
  • Timetable & Scheduling
  • Parent & Student Portal
  • Exam & Grading Management
  • Analytics & Reporting
